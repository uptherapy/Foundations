Foundations — a monthly developmental check-in, not a diagnosis.
Milestones reflect what most children do by a given age, based on the developmental checkpoints used in early intervention. Every child grows on their own timeline. If a strand stays thin, if your child loses a skill they once had, or if anything ever worries you, reach out to your pediatrician or your local Early Steps program — early support makes a real difference.
